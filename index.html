Kido cloud users

  • Administrators

    By creating a user friendly CRM, organize your system in a way that helps all the parties make an impact on the organization. Kido allows you to easily organize your classrooms, assign teachers, share messages, organize meals and to have an overview of the performance of the individual and the organization. Set your school apart with meticulously designed reports that will help your organization grow even further.

  • Teachers

    Will have the ability to organize children into classes, share valuable info between themselves, the rest of the school’s staff and the parents. They can easily send messages, share important class-wide announcements and more. In doing so, also help parents to have an active role in all of their children's victories. 

  • Parents

    Kido is built to keep parents fully informed about their children's progress and day-to-day activities; and to help them communicate with teachers and the staff by sharing information in real time.

  • Staff

    The chef needs to know who’s allergic to peanuts, or who doesn’t eat fish? The school bus driver needs to see tomorrow’s route? The administrators need to put a report together in a few clicks? We got you covered.

  • Support

     This is us! We have a team ready to assist with the set up and to provide answers to all of the questions you may have before and after your school has been enrolled. Just like parents, we’re there 24/7!
